Nano Banana2 is your ultimate AI image generation platform powered by Google's Gemini models. Create, edit, and inspire with cutting-edge AI technology.

## Key Features

### High-Quality Image Generation
Generate stunning images using the latest Gemini AI models. Our platform supports text-to-image and image-to-image generation with exceptional quality.

### Multiple Resolution Options
- **1K Resolution**: Quick generations for previews
- **2K Resolution**: Perfect for social media and web use
- **4K Resolution**: Professional-grade high-resolution output

### Simple Workflow
1. **Sign Up**: Create your account to get started
2. **Choose a Plan**: Select from our flexible pricing options
3. **Enter Your Prompt**: Describe what you want to create
4. **Generate & Download**: Get your images in seconds

## How Credits Work

Our credit system is simple and transparent:
- 1K Image: 10 Credits
- 2K Image: 10 Credits
- 4K Image: 20 Credits

Check your balance anytime at [/settings/credits](/settings/credits).
