# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: December 1, 2025

## 1. Applicability
This policy applies to your processing of personal information when you visit the **Nano Banana2** website/product and related services. It does not apply to third-party websites/services.

## 2. Information Controller
Currently responsible: **Nano Banana2** Team.
Contact: [support@nanobanana2.blog](mailto:support@nanobanana2.blog).
Location: Shenzhen, China.
If a company is established in the future, we will update this with the company entity and registered address.

## 3. Information Collected and Sources
- **Account Information**: Email, nickname/name (provided during registration or login).
- **Membership Status**: Free or paid membership tier, which determines available features (Free users: 1K resolution; Paid members: up to 2K resolution).
- **Transaction & Payment Information**: Payment credentials are processed by payment service providers; we only receive necessary transaction results. The actual payment processor is **Creem**.
- **Usage Data**: Feature usage, clicks, visited pages, timestamps.
- **Device/Logs**: IP address, browser/device type, operating system, crash logs.
- **Cookies/Local Storage**: Used for session maintenance, preferences, performance, and analytics.
- **Support & Communication**: Content you provide in tickets/emails.
- **Marketing Subscriptions**: Email and preferences you voluntarily provide.

## 4. Processing Purposes and Legal Basis
- **Provide/Maintain Service**: Contractual necessity.
- **Security & Anti-Abuse**: Legitimate interest.
- **Improvement & Analytics**: Legitimate interest/Consent.
- **Compliance/Tax Records**: Legal obligation.
- **Send Marketing Communications**: Consent (can be unsubscribed at any time).

## 5. Third Parties and Processors
We only allow the following third parties to process data according to our instructions, subject to confidentiality obligations:
- **Cloud Hosting**: [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
- **Payment**: [Creem](https://creem.io)
- **Email Sending**: [Resend](https://resend.com)
- **AI Models**: [Google Gemini](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/)
- **Analytics**: [Vercel Analytics](https://vercel.com/analytics)

## 6. International Transfers
Data may be transferred outside your country (including outside the US/EU). We use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards to ensure data security.

## 7. Cookies/Tracking
We use Cookies for necessary functionality, analytics, and marketing. You can refuse or withdraw consent via browser settings or the on-site "Cookie Settings".

## 8. Retention Period
- **Account Data**: Duration of account existence + reasonable backup period.
- **Transactions/Invoices**: 7 years (compliance with legal tax requirements).
- **Logs/Security Events**: 90-180 days.
- **Marketing Subscriptions**: Cleared after unsubscription or account deletion.
- **User-Generated Images**: Images you generate are stored for 30 days to allow you to access your history. After 30 days, images may be automatically deleted. You can also manually delete your images at any time from your account settings.
- **AI Processing**: When you submit prompts or images to our service, they are sent to Google Gemini API for processing. Google may retain this data according to their own privacy policy. We do not store your prompts or source images permanently on our servers.
- **Backups**: Automatically overwritten according to a rotation policy.

## 9. Security Measures
We employ transmission encryption, access control, and minimized access. While we cannot guarantee 100% security, we will notify you in accordance with legal requirements in the event of a major security incident.

## 10. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, delete, withdraw consent, and restrict/object to the processing of your personal data.
To exercise these rights: Email [support@nanobanana2.blog](mailto:support@nanobanana2.blog).
If you are located in the EU/UK/California, you also have specific rights granted by applicable laws.

## 11. Minors
This service is intended for users aged **18+**. We do not knowingly collect information from minors; if discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

## 12. Automated Decision Making and AI
- Generated content may contain errors and does not constitute professional advice.
- We are not responsible for legal/medical/financial consequences of automated outputs.
- **AI Training**: By default, we do **NOT** use your data to train our AI models unless you explicitly consent.
- **NSFW Content**: Our AI model (Google Gemini) is designed to refuse generating illegal, infringing, or NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content. If you attempt to generate such content, the request will be rejected by the AI model.

## 13. Policy Updates
Updates will be posted on this page with a "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be notified separately.
