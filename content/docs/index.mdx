## Overview
Nano Banana2 is your ultimate AI image generation platform powered by Google's Gemini models. Create stunning images in seconds, with support for 1K and 2K resolutions. Free users: 1K only, upgrade for 2K.

## Getting Started

### Sign in
Get started by signing in with your Google account at [/sign-in](/sign-in).

### Free Trial
New users can claim free credits to try out the service. Visit [/api/credits/free-trial](/api/credits/free-trial) or check the Pricing page.

### Generate
Go to [/ai-image-generator](/ai-image-generator), enter your prompt, select your preferred resolution, and click Generate. Your creation history is saved automatically.

## Billing & Plans

### Pricing
Visit [/pricing](/pricing) to view our flexible plans. We offer both subscription-based and pay-as-you-go (credits) options. Subscriptions renew automatically, while credit packs are one-time purchases.

### Payments
We accept major credit cards via Creem. You can find your invoices in the order details or via email receipt.

### Manage
Manage your subscription and payment methods at the [Billing Portal](/settings/billing). You can cancel your subscription or update your payment details there.

## Credits

### Consumption
- 1K Image: 10 Credits (Free users)
- 2K Image: 10 Credits (Members only)

### Balance
Check your current credit balance at [/settings/credits](/settings/credits).

## Refunds & Cancellation
